Our Story

The Power of Collective Farming

Chilli Producers Limited is more than just a farm; it is a collaborative powerhouse. By merging the expertise of over 100 serious Agribusiness (ABE) farmers, we eliminate the fragmentation risks typical of smallholder agriculture.

Our 20-hectare commercial production matrix in Lilongwe is engineered to bypass seasonal volatility. With state-of-the-art greenhouse setups and precision drip irrigation, we guarantee a 365-day continuous supply of premium grade chili to international markets.

Quality Guarantee

Strict phytosanitary standards from seed to export shelf.

Year-Round Supply

Climate-resilient infrastructure ensures zero off-seasons.

Product Portfolio

From Fresh Harvest to Premium Powders

We dominate the value chain by capturing premium margins. Half of our yield serves the fresh market, while the rest is processed into high-demand, shelf-stable derivatives.

Fresh Market

Premium Green Chilli

Harvested at peak crispness, sorted, and cold-packed specifically for retail and hospitality off-takers.

Export Grade

Premium Red Chilli

Carefully selected for color intensity, high Capsaicin content, and uniformity to meet international metrics.

Value Added

Dehydrated Powders

Finely milled red and green chili powders tailored for global spice blenders and food manufacturers.

Value Added

Chilli Flakes & Crushes

Coarsely crushed dried chilis, perfectly structured for fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) packaging.
