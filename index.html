Our Story

The Power of 100+ Farmers

Chilli Producers Limited is a premier, farmer-owned agribusiness enterprise based in Lilongwe, Malawi. Incorporated as a collaborative powerhouse by a group of more than 100 serious, highly-skilled ABE farmers, we are strategically positioned to become Malawi’s largest producer, value-adder, and exporter of premium chili products.

By merging commercial-scale production across a 20-hectare facility with state-of-the-art agricultural technologies, we guarantee a 365-day continuous supply of high-grade chili, bypassing seasonal volatility.

  • Commercial-scale production & security of supply.
  • Climate-resilient infrastructure (Greenhouse & Drip).
  • Strict international quality and sanitary standards.
